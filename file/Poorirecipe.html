Poori Recipe, How to make puri

Moreover, puri is made from whole wheat flour, oil, milk, salt and water. Which is popular for any time hunger.
There are many variations in puri like methi poori or masala poori or ajwain poori or stuffed poori, etc or you can also add pureed vegetables or grated vegetables while kneading the dough for more variations of poori.

Puri is made from whole wheat flour which is a puffed by frying.
Puri is an accomplishment that is going with any dishes for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

Puri Sabzi is another famous food of Odisha that is a breakfast staple.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ Cup Wheat Flour
  • 3 Tbsp Milk
  • 2 Tbsp Oil
  • Salt as per Taste
  • Water as Required
  • Oil for Deep Frying

Instructions

  1. Take a bowl and add wheat flour, oil, milk and salt. And mix all ingredients together.
  2. Now add water as required and knead slightly tight dough.
  3. Then add ½ tbsp oil and cover on dough. Furthermore, cover the dough with a cloth and rest for 15 minutes.
  4. Take prepare all small round shape poori dough from the dough.
  5. Now roll the dough in poori shape slightly thick.
  6. Prepare all poori like this. (This poori not need to rest. So when you complete 1 plate rolled poori then deep fry it.)
  7. When oil became medium hot then add poori in oil one by one. And lightly press on poori with a spoon or karchi so it’s puffed up.
  8. Fry poori golden brown from both sides.
  9. Now prepare all puri like this. And serve hot.
