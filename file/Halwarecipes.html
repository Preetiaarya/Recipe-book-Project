Sooji ka Halwa

You can make halwa in this way and enjoy the taste of home-made halwa even if you are away from home!

Simple ingredients creat this delicious and easy dish!

Whenever you feel like eating halwa,then definitely try this semolina halwa which can be prepared in very less time and will remind you of your home!
Only few minutes and it is ready: do you want to share somethingthis good?

Ingredients

  • Water-250ml
  • Sugar-100gm
  • Sooji-100gm
  • Desi ghee-100gm
  • Cardamon-1 tsp
  • Kaju and Badam-5-6 pcs
  • Kismis- tbsp

Procedure

  1. Take a pan and add water and sugar. Heat the pan on medium flame.
  2. Then take another pan, add Sooji and roast it for 3 minutes on medium flame. Once done,collect it in a bowl.
  3. Now add desi ghee in the same pan. After a minutes,add cardamon, Kaju,badam, and kismis and stir it for a few minutes.
  4. Add the roasted Sooji in the pan and cook it until it is combined with ghee
  5. Then add the sugar syrup into it, Stri the mixture well until the water is absorbed and it gets a more smooth texture. Server hot and enjoy with family.
