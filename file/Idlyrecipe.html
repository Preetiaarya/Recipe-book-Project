Kanchipuram Idly

Best south Indian Idly sambhar

Indulge in the rich heritage of South Indian cuisine with Kanchipuram Idly - a divine blend of tradition and taste.

Ingredients

  • Idly batter - 4 cups
  • Grated coconut - 1 cup
  • Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
  • Urad dal - 1 tsp
  • Gram dhal - 1tsp
  • Asafoetida - 1tsp
  • Red chilli - 8
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Oil - 4tsp
  • Salt as required

Method

  1. Take 2 tsp of oil in a pan add mustard let it splutter then add urad dal, gram dal, asafoetida one by one.
  2. Once the dal turns golden brown add grated coconut, salt, curry leaves and saute for a min.
  3. Add the sauted coconut mix with the batter and mix well.
  4. Now pour the batter in greased tumblers/ ss cups and steam cook them for 20min.
  5. Serve hot with your favourite chutney.
  6. Steaming tip - Steam the idlis in tumblers instead of idli plates.
  7. Scraping tip - Scrap the corners with knife before unmolding the idlis.
